Matrix(3905)
If you want to index a matrix:

Cols = 0  1  2  3  4
Row0 = 0  1  2  3  4
Row1 = 5  6  7  8  9
Row2 = 10 11 12 13 14

To create key for each index:
key = (row * m) + col

where m is no of cols = 5 above.
then to get
row = key / m;
col = key % m;
